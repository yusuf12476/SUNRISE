Our Story

Sunrise Chicken Farm blossomed from a heartfelt desire to bring genuinely fresh, wholesome chicken and eggs to our beloved community in Kakamega. Our journey began as a humble family endeavor, fueled by a profound love for farming and an unyielding dedication to quality.

Every single day, from the first rays of dawn, our singular focus is on cultivating a healthy, vibrant, and happy environment for our broilers and layers. We believe that well-cared-for birds naturally yield products of superior taste and nutrition – a difference you can truly savor.
