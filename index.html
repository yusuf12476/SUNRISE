Recipe Spotlight: Easy Farmhouse Roast Chicken

Published on: June 25, 2025

There's nothing quite like the aroma of a perfectly roasted chicken filling the house. With a Sunrise Chicken Farm broiler, you're already halfway to a memorable meal. This simple recipe enhances the natural, succulent flavor of our chicken.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Sunrise Chicken Farm Dressed Broiler (approx. 1.5-2kg)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 whole lemon, halved
  • 4-5 cloves of garlic, smashed
  • A few sprigs of fresh rosemary and thyme
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 onion, quartered, and 2 carrots, chopped, for the roasting pan

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 200°C (400°F). Pat the chicken dry inside and out with paper towels for extra crispy skin.
  2. Rub the entire chicken with olive oil. Season generously all over with salt and pepper.
  3. Stuff the cavity of the chicken with the halved lemon, smashed garlic cloves, and fresh herbs.
  4. If using, place the chopped onion and carrots in the bottom of a roasting pan to create a natural rack for the chicken. Place the chicken on top.
  5. Roast in the preheated oven for about 1 hour and 15 minutes, or until the juices run clear when a thigh is pierced and the internal temperature reaches 74°C (165°F).
  6. Let the chicken rest for 10-15 minutes before carving. This is crucial for a juicy result! Serve with the pan juices and your favorite sides. Enjoy!

The Incredible Health Benefits of Farm-Fresh Eggs

Published on: June 18, 2025

An egg is not just an egg, especially when it comes from a happy, healthy hen. Our farm-fresh eggs are a nutritional powerhouse, offering a wide array of benefits that make them a true superfood.

What's Inside a Sunrise Egg?

  • High-Quality Protein: With about 6 grams of protein per large egg, they are a perfect source of all nine essential amino acids, the building blocks of our bodies.
  • Rich in Vitamins: Our eggs are an excellent source of Vitamin D (crucial for bone health and immune function), Vitamin B12 (essential for nerve function and red blood cell formation), and Vitamin A.
  • Brain-Boosting Choline: Eggs are one of the best dietary sources of choline, a vital nutrient that plays a key role in brain development, memory, and liver function.
  • Heart-Healthy Fats: The fats found in eggs are primarily unsaturated and are important for a healthy heart and overall well-being.

When you choose Sunrise Chicken Farm eggs, you're not just getting a delicious ingredient; you're choosing a product packed with health benefits, laid by hens that are cared for ethically and fed a natural, wholesome diet. It's a difference you can see in the vibrant yolks and taste in every bite.
