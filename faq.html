Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, ordering, and delivery.

Our commitment is to quality and ethical farming. Our chickens are raised in spacious, clean environments and fed a natural, wholesome diet free from growth hormones. This results in healthier, more flavorful chicken and eggs with vibrant yolks. You can truly taste the difference!
You can easily place an order through our website! Simply browse our Products page, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. At checkout, you can fill in your details and send the order to us via WhatsApp or Email. You can also call us directly at 0793911383 to place your order.
We currently offer free delivery within Kakamega town and its immediate surroundings. For areas further out, a small delivery fee may apply. Please contact us with your location when placing an order, and we will confirm any delivery charges.
We accept several convenient payment methods:
  • M-Pesa (Paybill details provided on order confirmation)
  • Airtel Money
  • Cash on Delivery
  • PayPal
