Frequently Asked Questions

We specialize in farm-fresh dressed broiler chickens (offered whole), healthy live chickens (with both broiler and layer breeds available upon request), and nutrient-rich, farm-fresh eggs from our happy layer hens. Delivery & Order Tracking

Once you place your order through our form, WhatsApp, or phone call, we will confirm it promptly. Deliveries within Kakamega are typically completed within **2-4 hours** during business hours, depending on your exact location and current demand. We strive for the fastest possible delivery!

We will send you a confirmation message via WhatsApp or SMS once your order has been dispatched for delivery. Our delivery team may also call you when they are close to your location.

We recommend ensuring someone is available at the delivery address during the confirmed delivery window. If you anticipate not being home, please inform us in advance when placing your order, and we can try to arrange an alternative time or a safe drop-off point if suitable.
